  • Model United Nations, also known as Model UN or MUN, is an educational simulation and/or academic activity in which students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. MUN involves and teaches participants speaking, debating, and writing skills, in addition to critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership abilities.Usually an extracurricular activity, some schools also offer Model UN as a class. It is meant to engage students and allow them to develop deeper understanding into current world issues.

  • A visit to the 38th Session of the Humans Rights Council

    • 28 june 2018

    On June 21st, I had the opportunity to observe the 38th Session of the Human Rights Council in Geneva.

    A visit to the 38th Session of the Humans Rights Council
    Upon arrival at Room XX at Palais des Nations, a fellow intern and I took our seats behind representatives from Seychelles and Singapore, underneath the iconic ceiling sculpture that encompasses the room. The session commenced with an invitation from the President of the HRC, H.E. Mr. Vojislav Šuc, for countries to join the speakers’ list. Representatives rapidly clicked red buttons on their displays to try and secure a favorable position on the list. The High Commissioner for Human Rights, Zeid Raad Al Hussein, then addressed the manner in which digital media has opened the door for new forms of oppression and violence committed against women. He discussed several cases in which female human rights defenders have fallen victim to harassment and defamation online, and encouraged states to find concrete ways to prevent human rights abuses from being committed in the future.
  • Oh, the places you will'go..

    • 19 june 2018

    With conferences spanning three continents and attracting students from over 85 nationalities, WIMUN participants have the opportunity to travel the world while forging international friendships.

    Modal title
    New York City, USA (WIMUN Flagship) New York City is home to the notorious Statue of Liberty, Brooklyn Bridge, Central Park, and, most importantly, WIMUN’s flagship conference. The UN headquarters are nestled in the Turtle Bay neighborhood of Manhattan and host the opening ceremony of the conference. The city is the perfect location for delegates who want to experience the hustle and bustle of a city comprised of more than 8 million people from all nationalities. The central location of the Grand Hyatt, which houses WIMUN delegates, goes unrivaled. The hotel is within walking distance to some of New York’s most recognizable landmarks including Times Square, Rockefeller Center, and the Empire State Building. For those looking to learn more about the events of 9/11, its victims, survivors, and lasting impact should visit the 9/11 Memorial Plaza and Museum. Before departing, visitors should set aside time to ride the 22 mph elevator to the 100th floor of the One World Trade Center for a panoramic view of the city. For a bite to eat in the Big Apple, Artichoke Pizza and Joe’s are just two of many classic New York style pizzerias recommended by locals. Delegates to WIMUN NY can experience the city through cuisine, sightseeing tours, and walks in the park, so get ready to explore the city’s many skyscraper lined street to see what can be discovered. Campinas, Brazil (WIMUN Brazil) Campinas’ ascension to Brazil’s main high-tech center and central location in Sao Paulo made the city a perfect contender to host WIMUN conferences. The conference is held at the vibrant orange FACAMP University campus, a mere 15 minutes from downtown. Student life is prevalent throughout Campinas thanks to its numerous universities and their close proximity to the city center. Situated in the South East of Brazil, this city provides a culturally enriching environment different than that of any other Brazilian state. The Central Market offers any passerby a taste of Brazil’s freshest produce and local dishes. A trip to Campinas or any part of Sao Paulo cannot be completed before trying a Coxinha, a tear-drop pastry with fillings ranging from cheese to chicken. People who prefer the outdoors over crowded city streets will enjoy cooling off at Parque do Taquaral and renting a swan=shaped paddleboat at the lake located therein. As another hub for international business, one can understand how Sao Paulo earned its nickname “New York of Brazil” and why this innovative economic states was selected as a destination for WIMUN delegates. WIMUN Brazil 2019 is quickly approaching, so make sure to register now to avoid missing out on an incredible trip! Agra, India (WIMUN India) Agra, a city in the Uttar Pradesh State, is accustomed to its fair share of tourist looking to get a glimpse of one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Jaypee Palace is just 10 minutes by car from the monument and provides an elegant backdrop for this conference. A visit to the 370 year old Taj Mahal is arranged for all conference delegates, so everyone will have the opportunity to see this incredible landmark. While at the Taj Mahal, it is worth taking a stroll through the Mehtab Bagh, or Moonlight Garden, where you can pause for a photo op with other delegates. Although Agra is best known for this site, the city has plenty to offer. For more architectural marvels, the marble and red sandstone fort and Akbar’s tomb are two more impressive structures to see. The train ride to New Delhi is also a great idea for anyone with a little more time to travel within India. Several local foods including Petha, a soft candy made from ash gourd vegetable; Paratha, layers of cooked dough; and Jalebi and Kachori, popular snacks, are all necessary to try while staying in Agra. Our 2018 conference in Agra just concluded with a superhero gala and consensus from all committees, and we hope future conferences will be met with similar excitement and success.

BEST DELEGATE

Best Delegate is an education organization with a mission to help students and teachers succeed in Model United Nations worldwide. Best Delegate runs the Model United Nations Institute and has conducted workshops in North America, Latin America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. Its website and online resources are used by over 650,000 participants annually from over 190 countries and territories. Best Delegate has helped organize nearly a dozen conferences at the UN Headquarters and is a pioneer in implementing WIMUN Approach simulations. Best Delegate oversees the conference planning and management for WIMUN New York.

MUNcafe

MUNCafé, a brand of Worldview Education, is a global platform providing opportunities and solutions for students interested in experiential learning through Model United Nations and other inter-governmental simulations. We use a mix of travel, conferences, projects, training & mentorship modules as tools in our international experiential learning programs. Our aim is to develop responsible global leaders through impactful experiential learning. MUNCafé is our partner in hosting WIMUN India in addition to being the Outreach Consultant for WIMUN New York for the regions of India, Middle-East, and South Asia.
